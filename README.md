# Bingo Gacha
Introduce a fun, engaging, and addictive gameplay loop to your server! **Bingo Gacha** gives players their own interactive progression cards where they roll random slots to build lines and win epic custom rewards.

---

## How It Works (For Players)

1. **Get a Card:** Open the card menu `/bingo` and purchase or claim a card.
2. **Roll the Gacha:** Pay server money (Vault) or items to roll. Watch the board spin with custom flickering glass panes and sound effects as it picks your next slot!
3. **Get a Bingo:** Connect lines horizontally, vertically, or diagonally (or unlock the entire board) to claim completion rewards.
4. **Extra Rewards:** Any slots left unrolled when you complete a straight line can grant bonus prizes!

---

## Features for Server Owners

*   **Highly Addictive Loop:** Gives players a fun place to spend their extra currency or materials.
*   **Voxel-Style UI Animations:** Premium inventory menus featuring custom colored glass pane roll animations and sound cues.
*   **3x3 and 5x5 Boards:** Create quick-to-complete boards or massive long-term progression challenges.
*   **Custom Roll Costs & Prizes:** Configure templates with custom entry fees and reward commands (items, money, XP, etc.).
*   **Anti-Spam Cooldowns:** Keep rolls fair and prevent menu spamming.
